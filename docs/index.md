# Word of the Day

**Learn a new word every day, right inside Obsidian.**

Word of the Day fetches [Merriam-Webster's Word of the Day](https://www.merriam-webster.com/word-of-the-day) and saves it as a clean, structured note in your vault. Optionally generate spaced-repetition flashcards with [Osmosis](https://github.com/SawyerRensel/Osmosis) so you actually remember what you learn.

## What It Does

1. **Fetches** the Word of the Day from Merriam-Webster's RSS feed
2. **Creates** a note in your vault with pronunciation, definition, examples, and etymology
3. **Optionally embeds** an Osmosis flashcard so you can study the word with spaced repetition

Each word gets its own file -- no date prefixes, no clutter. Just `fiscal.md`, `ephemeral.md`, `serendipity.md`.
